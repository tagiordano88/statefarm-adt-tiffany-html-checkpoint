North Texas Travel Hockey

Hockey is pretty popular in Texas. There are several teams just in the DFW area. There are also several different levels of teams as well. There is a beginners league called a "house" league, high school league, and travel leagues.

The competition to get on one of the travel teams is high and you have to start early. Players start by attending open skates and camps during the summer. Then there are tryouts to see if you can make the cut.

Here are a list of some the travel teams inside the DFW area:

  • McKinney North Stars
  • Texas Warriors
  • Dallas Penguins
  • Texas Tigers
