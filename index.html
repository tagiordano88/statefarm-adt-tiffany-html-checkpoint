North Texas Travel Hockey

Hockey is pretty popular in Texas. There are several teams just in the DFW area. The competition to get on one of these teams is high and you have to start early. Players start by attending open skates and camps during the summer. Then there are tryouts to see if you can make the cut.

Here are a list of some the teams inside the DFW area:

  • McKinney North Stars
  • Texas Warriors
  • Dallas Penguins
  • Texas Tigers
